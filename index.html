    **Our availability for jobs may be subject to change based on other bookings, weather, and sunset/sunrise times**

    Based on regulations from the FAA and capabilities of our drones there may be days where we are not available based on weather. Weather is an unfortunate enemy of ours and conditions that will result in us not being able to fly include but are not limited to:

  • Rain
  • Clouds lower than 800 feet AGL
  • Snow
  • Winds exceeding 22 Knots (gusting or consistent speed)
